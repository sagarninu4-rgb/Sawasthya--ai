#  Swasthya AI – Intelligent Healthcare Monitoring System

Swasthya AI is an AI-powered healthcare platform built to help people understand their health symptoms and find nearby medical facilities quickly. The goal of this project is to make early health guidance more accessible using artificial intelligence.

##  The Problem

Many people ignore or misunderstand early symptoms of illnesses. Because of this, they often delay visiting a doctor. Another common issue is that people don't always know where the nearest healthcare facilities are, especially in urgent situations.

## Our Solution

Swasthya AI provides a simple and smart way for users to check their symptoms and get AI-generated health insights. By selecting their symptoms, users can receive possible health suggestions and guidance on what to do next. The platform also helps users locate nearby hospitals using geolocation.

To make the experience more interactive and user-friendly, the platform includes an AI voice assistant and visual 3D medical elements.

## Key Features

* AI-powered symptom analyzer
* Secure login using Google authentication
* Automatic patient ID sent through email
* AI-based health suggestions
* Live hospital map with geolocation
* AI voice assistant for interaction
* Interactive 3D medical animations
